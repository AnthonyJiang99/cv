Yonder is a student organization on campus that strives to compete at the University Rover Challenge, where we compete against other university teams to build a Mars Rover that can complete different tasks, such as manipulating human tools, taking soil samples, and using autonomous navigation.

AMAEzon delivery contest

End of quarter competition for MAE 3. The Spring 2018 challenge was to design and build a robot to move wooden blocks from one side of the play field to scoring zones on the other side. My team placed 3rd at the end of year bracket.

World Robot Olympiad

WRO is a LEGO robotics competition I participated in during junior high. The 2013 challenge was to design and build an autonomous robot to deliver the "broken" statue back to an end zone, while leaving the other 3 "unbroken" statues within their own starting squares. I placed first in the Hsin-Chu regional competition before moving back to the States.
